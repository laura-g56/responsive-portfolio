About me

Laura Gumienik

Creative | Passionate | Intuitive


Hello and welcome to my website!
My name is Laura and I am based in Victoria, Australia.

Despite graduating with a Diploma of Interior Design & Decoration, I currently work as a full time civil and structural Engineering draftsperson. However, having always been interested in coding, with limited experience as part of my high school studies, I decided to try my hand at coding via SheCodes - an introduction to coding for women via online lessons.

Given how much I am loving to learn to code and once again embrace my creative side, I am open to the idea of working as a full-time front-end developer, as a pivot in my career. I am a logical problem solver - as well as a creative - who is yearning to mesh these two passions together to produce efficient and engaging content for a memorable user experience.

If you like my work and would like to chat, feel free to contact me!
