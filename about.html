About me

Laura Gumienik


Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Laura and I was born in 1986 in Geelong, Australia.

After finishing high school, I studied Civil Engineering at Monash University in Melbourne but was unfulfilled. I took a semester off to think about my future before deciding upon my passion of interior design. I graduated RMIT with a diploma in Interior Decoration and Design and worked in the field for a few years part time. Despite enjoying the experience immensely, I couldn't secure full time employment so looked elsewhere and stumbled back across to Engineering but this time as a drafsperson.

I currently work as a full time civil and structural draftsperson, however having always been interested in coding, with limited experience as part of my high school studies, I decided to try my hand at coding via SheCodes - an intro to coding via online classes. I guess the global Covid pandemic made this decision easier, as I finally had time to focus on myself throughout the many weeks of lockdowns Victoria imposed on it's citizens.
